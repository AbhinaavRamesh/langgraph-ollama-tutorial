# Learning Progress

Track your progress through the LocalGraph tutorials, review quiz performance, and identify areas for improvement.

<QuizDashboard />

## About This Dashboard

This dashboard provides comprehensive insights into your learning journey:

### Statistics Tracked
- **Total Questions Attempted**: All quiz questions you've answered across all tutorials
- **Overall Accuracy**: Your average success rate across all quizzes
- **Average Time Per Question**: How quickly you complete quiz questions
- **Questions to Review**: Questions scheduled for review based on spaced repetition

### Phase Breakdown
View your progress and performance for each learning phase:
- **Core Patterns**: Fundamental LangGraph concepts and patterns
- **RAG Patterns**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation techniques
- **Multi-Agent**: Multi-agent collaboration patterns
- **Advanced**: Advanced agentic patterns and strategies

### Learning Features

#### Spaced Repetition
Questions are scheduled for review based on your performance:
- Questions answered incorrectly are reviewed daily
- Correctly answered questions are reviewed at increasing intervals (1, 3, 7, 14 days)
- This helps reinforce learning and improve long-term retention

#### Weak Areas Identification
The dashboard automatically identifies questions where you've struggled:
- Questions with 2+ incorrect attempts are flagged
- Suggested tutorials to revisit for better understanding
- Review mode to focus on challenging concepts

#### Achievement System
Earn badges as you progress:
- First Steps: Complete your first quiz
- Perfect Score: Achieve 100% accuracy on any phase
- Speed Demon: Average less than 10 seconds per question
- Week Warrior: Maintain a 7-day learning streak
- Completionist: Complete all tutorials in a phase
- Master Learner: Complete all tutorials
- Accuracy Ace: Achieve 90% accuracy with 20+ attempts
- Persistent: Attempt 100 total questions

#### Streak Counter
Stay motivated with daily streak tracking:
- Current streak shows consecutive days with quiz activity
- Best streak displays your longest learning streak
- Encourages consistent learning habits

## Tips for Success

1. **Review Regularly**: Check your dashboard to identify weak areas
2. **Use Review Mode**: Focus on questions you've struggled with
3. **Maintain Your Streak**: Daily practice leads to better retention
4. **Aim for Accuracy**: Quality over quantity - understand concepts thoroughly
5. **Complete All Phases**: Build a solid foundation by progressing through all tutorials

## Privacy Note

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Your progress is private and stays on your device.
